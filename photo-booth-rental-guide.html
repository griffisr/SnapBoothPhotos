Photo Booth Rental Guide

Navigating all of the dos and don'ts of renting a Photo Booth for your event can be stressful and difficult. Luckily, we've put together a guide to help you pick the right booth for you.

  • First Steps

    Submit a request to see if the date of your event is available. It's best to check availability even if you're in the early stages of planning as reservations are regularly booked.

  • PICK YOUR PACKAGE & RECEIVE YOUR FREE QUOTE

    Choose a package that's just right for your event! Decide how long you'd like your booth, choose your print style (classic 2X6 photo strips or 4X6 portraits), and add any add-ons!

  • RESERVE YOUR BOOTH

    Once you've picked your package and decided the details of your event, we'll put everything in writing for you to reserve your booth with a deposit and signed contract.

  • CUSTOMIZE CUSTOMIZE CUSTOMIZE

    After your booth is reserved, we'll work with you to create your entirely custom experience. From designing a personalized logo for your prints, to custom screens for your booth, Snap Booth Photos does it all to ensure your photo booth eperience is top notch!
